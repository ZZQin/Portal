7 Unique Panel Styles

The Classic Collection offers seven distinct panel styles, each designed to complement different architectural aesthetics. traditional raised panels to modern flush designs, find the perfect look for your home.

Short

Traditional short panel design with classic raised profile, perfect for colonial and traditional homes.

Long

Elongated panel design that creates a sense of height and elegance for larger door openings.

Short-V-Groove

Short panels with distinctive V-shaped groove detailing for added visual interest and texture.

Long-V-Groove

Extended panels featuring elegant V-groove patterns that enhance contemporary architectural styles.

Recessed

Sophisticated recessed panel design offering a refined, architectural look with subtle depth.

Flush

Clean, smooth surface ideal for modern and minimalist home designs with understated elegance.

Ribbed

Horizontal ribbed texture that adds dimension and character, perfect for industrial or rustic aesthetics.

Panel Structure Options

Each Classic Collection door can be configured with different panel structures to achieve the exact look and performance you need. Our innovative construction techniques ensure lasting durability and visual appeal.

Single-Layer Steel

Durable single-layer steel construction offering excellent value and reliable performance for standard applications.

Double-Layer Steel

Steel front with polyurethane backer providing enhanced insulation and noise reduction for improved comfort.

Triple-Layer Steel

Premium steel sandwich construction with thick polyurethane core delivering maximum insulation and strength.

2" Thick Insulated

Industry-leading panel thickness with high-density insulation for superior thermal performance and structural integrity.

Galvanized Steel

Corrosion-resistant galvanized steel construction ideal for coastal or high-humidity environments.

Premium Finish

Multi-layer protective coating system with UV-resistant topcoat for lasting color retention and protection.
