Our Story

The Beginning

Built by makers,
for makers

PORTAL was founded on a simple belief: the garage isn't just storage. It's where potential becomes real. Every revolutionary company—Apple, HP, Dyson—started in a garage with nothing but conviction.

We set out to build garage doors worthy of those spaces. Doors that don't just open and close, but that mark the threshold between dreaming and doing.

Today, we offer three tiers of garage doors—ONE, CRAFT, and LEGACY—each designed for different journeys, but all built with the same commitment to quality and purpose.
