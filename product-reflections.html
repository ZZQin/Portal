Sleek Sophistication Meets Modern Performance

The Reflections Collection embodies contemporary elegance with its smooth, flush panel design. Each door creates a mirror-like surface that beautifully reflects its surroundings, making it the perfect complement to modern architectural aesthetics while delivering superior thermal performance and durability.

Three-Layer Thermal Break Design

Crafted with inner and outer hot-dipped galvanized steel sheets coated with colorful, roller-applied finishes, and filled with eco-friendly, high-density polyurethane foam.

Superior Insulation Performance

Classic three-layer insulated door panel design, filled with closed-cell polyurethane foam, significantly improves home energy efficiency.

Customizable Panel Thickness

Available in two panel thickness options, 1-9/16" and 2", to meet the needs of different homes and climates.

Versatile Window Designs

Kosper offers a variety of window styles and designs to complement different garage door aesthetics.

Customize Your Perfect Door

Personalize your Reflections door with our extensive selection of colors, window styles, and glass options to complement your home's modern character.
