FAQ

Common questions

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed in 4-6 hours. Legacy custom doors may require additional time for precise fitting.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options for all three tiers. Contact us to learn more about payment plans.

What's included in the warranty?

Portal One includes a 10-year materials warranty. Portal Craft extends that to 15 years. Portal Legacy includes a lifetime craftsmanship warranty.

Can I see the doors in person?

Absolutely. Visit our showroom at 367 Addison Avenue in Palo Alto, or schedule a consultation for an in-home viewing.

Do you service my area?

We currently serve the greater Bay Area, Los Angeles, and San Diego. Contact us to confirm availability in your location.
